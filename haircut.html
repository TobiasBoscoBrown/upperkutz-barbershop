Men's Haircut

The bread and butter. A men's haircut at UpperKutz starts with a quick chat about what you want, then Travis cuts it to suit your hair, your face, and how much upkeep you want. Clippers, scissors, or both, with the sides taken as short or as long as you like and the top shaped for easy everyday styling.
